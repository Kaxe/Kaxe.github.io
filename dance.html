Dance Dance Revolution Replica

Dance Dance Revolution was originally a Japanese arcade game. A replica of the the game was recreated with DE2 board and hardware programming. The game utilized custom made dance pads to take inputs of the pulse, meanwhile VGR and speakers were responsible for the visual and audio outputs.
