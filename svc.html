RCBB Social Venture Challenge

Rotman Commerce Beyond Business Social Venture Challenge

A social venture is a profitable organization that is aiming to solve social issues and improve people's living in general. I teamed up with 2 other students to join the competition and presented our idea called EXO. EXO was a device that could detect head motion of a paralysed patient and give commands to electronics. We did not win the competition but we received positive feedbacks from the judges.
